The New World

Appetizer


Tortilla Chips and Guacamole

Ingredients: Avacado, Tortilla Chips

Description: Altough it isn't clear when Tortilla Chips and Guacamole originated, we know that they both originated in the Mesoamerica region The most basic version of the dish uses corn flour to make tortillas, chops them up into smaller pieces, fry them and then dip in squashed Avacado. They were first mass produced originally in LA in the 1940s but we don't exactly know who started it

Main Course


Tacos al Pastor

Ingredients: Turkey, Pork, Corn Tortillas, Pinapples, Onions, Cilantro, Lime

Description: Tacos al pastor is a Mexican dish that originated in early 20th centry and is said to be influenced by Greek Gyro, the original version of the dish ued Turkey as its main meat and didn't use Cilantro, Lime, and Onions but as people started eating it they also started replacing the Turkey with pork and added Cilantro and Lime as it made it taste better

Ingredients: Sweet Potatoes, Vanilla Extract, Eggs, Milk

Description: Sweet Potatoes pie is belived to have gernerated somewhere in southern United States and Northern Mexico, it was inspired from Traditional English and West African Pies but made with ingredients only found in the New World. The exact date of its origin is unknown too but it is said to be originated somewher in the late 1700s and early 1800s
